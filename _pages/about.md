I am a Research Associate at the [CVI² Lab](https://www.uni.lu/snt-en/people/abid-ali/)￼, SnT￼, University of Luxembourg, working on facial deepfake detection and spacecraft pose estimation using deep learning and computer vision.

I completed my Ph.D. at [INRIA](https://team.inria.fr/stars/en/)￼, Sophia Antipolis, and Côte d’Azur University, supervised by Dr. François Brémond and Dr. Susanne Thümmler, with support from the [Marie Curie Co-Found Fellowship](https://univ-cotedazur.eu/boosturcareer/project-act4autism). My research spans video understanding, human behavior analysis, gaze estimation, and applied machine learning. I hold an M.S. from Sejong University and a B.S. from COMSATS University Islamabad.

Outside of work, I enjoy hiking, swimming, and traveling to explore new cultures and landscapes.
